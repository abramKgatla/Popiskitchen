Welcome

We bake all sorts of delicious cookies and cook a great variety of food to cater for your small to large functions. Popi's Kitchen is based in Atteridgeville, we have been selling tasty cookies in Pretoria and surrounding areas for the past five years and we never get tired of baking because if there is one thing we love in this world is food. Our passion for food is strong and our happy customers who keep comming back for more cookies, scones and biscuits. Our cookie packages are convenient for when you are hosting a function or when you want to show some support to a friend or family member who is having a function. Cookie deliveries are made for a small additional fee starting from R20 depending on how far away from our kitchen you want your cookies to be delivered

Hosting a party or function can be very stressful event. the are a lot of issues that need your attention. Things such as buying groceries for your event, making sure that the food you buy will be enough for everyone, transporting the large amount of food from the store to your event in time, preparing food for hundreds of guests, organising people who will help you prepare the food and other people who will serve your guests,making sure that the food tastes good and is ready on time. cleaning the dishes when every one has finished eating.

You need our food catering, when you book us to cater at your event all of the above mentioned issues will be profesionaly taken care of. We will cook delicious menus of your choosing at an affordable price for your business functions, weddings, funerals all other types of functions you can think of.When it comes to food catering delivery costs are included in the total cost of catering at your event.
